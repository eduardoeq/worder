How to play Guess the word in six tries. Each guess must be a valid five-letter word. Hit the "Try" button to submit. After each guess, the color of the tiles will change to show how close your guess was to the word. Examples: C R A T E   The letter C is in the word and in the correct spot. F A L L S   The letter A is in the word but in the wrong spot. M I N U S   The letter U is not in the word in any spot.
